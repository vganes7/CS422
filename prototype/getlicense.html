First Time Illinois Drivers



All residents using Illinois DMV are required state law to provide honest and transparent documentation.

Illinois Adults



1. Complete an approved Adult Driver Education 6-hour course.

2. Submit required documentation and required photo.

3. Pass the required tests.

---vision screening

---written exam

---behind the wheel exam

4. Pay the required fees.



Illinois Teens



1. Teens under 18 must be enrolled in a state approved driver education program.

---Driver Ed courses consist of 30 hours of classroom instruction and 6 hours behind the wheel.

2. Proof of Driver Enrollment Program.

3. Submit required documentation and required photo.

4. Proof of Permit.

5. Pass the behind the wheel test.
