Get a State Identification Card


All residents must submit the required documents to be considered for a state ID.

All documents must be original. Copies of documents will not be allowed. The system and

DMV representatives will verify originality of documents.

All documents will be submitted as proof of residency under Illinois law.


You must show proof a Social Security Number

Acceptable documents include Social Security card, military record, or driver's license record.


You must show your date of birth.

Acceptable documents include birth certificate, passport, or high school record.

Non-US documents are accepted as long as date of birth is accurate.


You must show proof of residency.

Acceptable documents include utility bill, government issued letter, or a bank statement.

To become a full time resident of Illinois, you must maintain your residence for at least one full year.




Documentation that has been submitted for approval on weekdays from 8am-5pm will be approved within 30 minutes of submission. Submissions outside of that time frame and/or on state/federal holidays will be approved within 2-3 business days.
