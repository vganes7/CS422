Illinois Teen Driver's License Documentation Upload

Please upload your completion certificate from your state approved Driver's Education Program.

The documentation must verify that you have completed 30 hours of classroom instruction and that

you have completed 6 hours of behind the wheel instruction.



Please upload a photo of yourself taken in front a blue colored background.



Please input your permit number

Please schedule an appointment to take your behind the wheel exam with a DMV representative.






Documentation that has been submitted for approval on weekdays from 8am-5pm will be approved within

30 minutes of submission. Submissions outside of that time frame and/or on state/federal holidays will

be approved within 2-3 business days.
