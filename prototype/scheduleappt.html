Schedule an appointment with a DMV representative


Please use the controls to schedule a 30 minute appointment with a DMV representative

All appointments are weekdays only from 8am-5pm. Please plan appropriately.

Once you schedule an appointment, you will receive a confirmation email. A DMV

representative will be contacted confirming your appointment.

You may cancel your appointment at any time and your DMV representative will be alerted.

Multiple appointments are allowed if necessary.
